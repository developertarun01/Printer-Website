Yes. Cookies are small text files stored on your computer’s hard drive through your web browser (with your consent). They help our website recognize your browser and retain certain information for future reference.

You can disable cookies from your browser settings if you prefer; however, doing so might affect some site features or reduce your overall browsing experience.

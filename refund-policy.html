Refund and Cancellation Policy

At printerbuddies.online, customer satisfaction remains our highest priority. This Refund and Cancellation Policy describes the conditions under which cancellations, returns, or refunds can be processed, in accordance with the Consumer Protection Act, 2019 and other relevant Indian laws.

Order Cancellation Policy

A. Before Shipment

You may cancel your order before dispatch by reaching out to our support team at: contact@printerbuddies.online.
If your order is cancelled prior to shipping, a complete refund will be issued within 5–7 business days to your original mode of payment.

B. After Shipment

Once an order has been shipped, cancellation is no longer possible. You may refuse delivery, in which case the product will be returned to us. Refunds will be initiated once we receive the returned product in its original condition (refer to Refund Terms below).

Return & Replacement Policy

A. Eligibility

Returns and replacements are accepted only under the following circumstances:

  • The product received is defective, damaged, or incorrect.
  • The product remains unused, unopened, and returned in its original packaging along with all manuals, accessories, and invoices.

B. Timeframe

A return request must be submitted within 7 days of delivery. To initiate a return or replacement, please contact our support team with your Order ID, product images, and a brief reason for return.

C. Non-Returnable Items

The following products are not eligible for return:

  • Items damaged due to mishandling or misuse.
  • Software, digital downloads, or license-based products.
  • Opened or used ink/toner cartridges.
  • Customized or special-order items.

Refund Policy

A. Refund Method

After your return request is approved and the item has been inspected, the refund will be processed through:

  • The original payment mode (Credit/Debit Card, UPI, Net Banking, etc.).
  • Refunds are usually completed within 7–10 business days after product verification.

B. Deductions

If any returned product lacks its original packaging, accessories, or shows visible signs of use, a partial refund or restocking charge may apply.

Shipping for Returns

We offer complimentary reverse pickup for eligible return cases. In regions where reverse pickup is unavailable, customers may be required to send the product to our return address. Reasonable shipping costs will be reimbursed in confirmed cases involving defective or damaged items.

Dispute Resolution

All disputes will be handled in accordance with applicable consumer protection laws within India.
